About Us

The Open Resource Exchange was developed by the City of Montreal in collaboration with the Government of Canada and the City of Edmonton. It provides a platform for sharing innovative ideas across all levels of government in Canada. This resource platform creates a gathering space for ‘open source’ ideas across Canada. It enables public administrators to:

  • Share open source solutions that can be replicable across Canada.
  • Download working open source solutions and execute in new environments.
  • Contribute to existing open source solutions.
  • Learn about open source standards, software and documentation.
