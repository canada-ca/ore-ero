Open Research

Open research refers to any research where anyone with relevant expertise is allowed to contribute and the resulting data is available to everyone online.

Search the catalog for research by different levels of Canadian public administrations.

You may also add a research or update its data using using the Open Research Form.

Need help? Check our user guide on how to use the Open Resource Exchange platform.
