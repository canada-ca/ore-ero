Open Standards

Open standards refer to file formats, protocols and application interfaces that can be implemented by everyone. The standard specification is publically available and the standardization is open and transparent.

Software Projects

Search open standards that are used by different levels of Canadian public administrations (federal, provincial, municipal and aboriginal). You may also add a new open standard to the following catalog, or update the information of a standard that is already there, using the Open Standards Form.
